DMCA Copyright Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Developer Bilaspur doosron ke intellectual property rights aur copyright parameters ki respect karta hai. Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) ke safe harbor rules ke antargat, hum copyright infringement reports ko prompt handle karte hain.

Pro Tip: Agar aap content removals ke liye notice file kar rahe hain, toh complete proof documents attach karna mandatory hai.

1. Filing an Infringement Claim

Agar aapko lagta hai ki is blog site par aapka copyrighted material (images, text descriptions, codes) bina permissions ke post kiya gaya hai, toh aap hume copyright takedown notice send kar sakte hain. Aapke notice me nimn details honi chahiye:

  • Aapke original copyrighted content ka URL ya sample.
  • Developer Bilaspur site par infringing material ka precise URL path.
  • Aapka signature aur validation details (email aur mobile phone number).

2. Takedown Actions

Valid DMCA takedown claim receive hotey hi, hamari support team verification karke 48 hours ke andar related images ya content paragraphs ko site se delete/remove kar degi.

3. DMCA Agent Contact

Copyright takedown notifications ko process karne ke liye hamara direct email contact hai:
