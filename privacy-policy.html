Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Developer Bilaspur ("hum", "humara" ya "us") aapki privacy ka poora dhyan rakhta hai. Yeh Privacy Policy outlines karti hai ki hum aapki information ko kaise collect, use aur protect karte hain jab aap humari website developerbilaspur.in ko visit karte hain.

Pro Tip: Humare traffic analytics parameters completely anonymized hain aur security SSL protocols (HTTPS) dwara protect kiye jate hain.

1. Information Collection

Hum nimnlikhit tarah ki details collect kar sakte hain:

  • Personal Data: Jab aap WhatsApp buttons par click karke hume message send karte hain ya blog update subscribe karte hain, tabhi humara system aapka phone number ya email fetch karta hai.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Hum browser performance aur traffic insights ke liye basic local cache, session variables, aur Google Analytics tracking metrics use karte hain.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collect ki gayi information ka upyog hum sirf in baaton ke liye karte hain:

  • Aapke businesses ke liye customize web structures ready karne.
  • Blog publishing systems aur newsletters ko secure rakhne.
  • Aapke technical questions aur support inquiries ka WhatsApp ya email dwara reply karne.

3. Data Protection & Security

Aapka data hamare security parameters ke hisab se safe rehta hai. Hum kisi bhi third-party networks ko aapka private data bechte ya share nahi karte hain. Security aur local storage protocols ko secure connections (HTTPS) ke sath treat kiya jata hai.
